Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A childhood classic! This is one of the simplest meals you can make, but it's delicious. Endless variations exist, you can use any number of breads, jams, jellies or even substitute something else for jelly, like honey! Additionally, you can even fry it!

Ingredients

  • Choice of bread
  • Choice of jelly
  • Choice of peanut butter

Steps

  1. Take your first slice of bread, spread a thin layer of peanut butter on one side of it
  2. Take your second slice of bread, spread a thin layer of peanut butter on one side of it
  3. Take about a tablespoon of jelly, and spread it on top of one of the layers of peanut butter
  4. Put the two pieces of bread together, making sure to keep all of the peanut butter and jelly to the interior of the sandwich
  5. Optionally, you may pan fry this sandwich in butter or a neutral oil
