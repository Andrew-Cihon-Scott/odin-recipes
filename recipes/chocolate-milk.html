Chocolate Milk

Description

A delicious drink to be enjoyed at all ages!

Ingredients

  • Non flavored milk of choice (Cow, Oat, Almond, etc.)
  • Chocolate Syrup

Steps

  1. Pour a large glass of milk for yourself
  2. Pour the chocolate syrup into the milk. Pour as much as you want! Go crazy!
  3. Give it a good stir
  4. Enjoy!
